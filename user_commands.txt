plot [plot-string] ~colour ~size
    plots the graph plot-string with the colour and size. the colour and size parameters are preceded with a tilde (~).
    the colour and size values can take default values.
    can also take the shorthand "pl".

move [dx] [dy]
    moves the centre of the graph by dx in the x direction and by dy in the y direction.
    can also take the shorthand "mv".

scale [kx] [ky] / scale [k]
    scales graph by kx in the x and ky in the y axis respectively. alternatively, scales both axes by k.
    can also take the shorthand "sc".

colour [plot-id] [new-colour]
    changes the colour of the plot at id plot-id.
    can also take the shorthand "cl".

size [plot-id] [new-size]
    changes the line size of the plot at plot-id.
    can also take the shorthand "sz".

swap [plot-id-1] [plot-id-2]
    swap the order of two plots plot-id-1 and plot-id-2
    can also take the shorthand "sw".

back [plot-id]
    pushes the plot in plot-id back one spot.
    can also take the shorthand "bk".

front [plot-id]
    pushes the plot in plot-id forward one spot.
    can also take the shorthand "fr".

remove [plot-id]
    removes the plot at plot-id.
    can also take the shorthand "rm".

removeall
    removes all plots.
    can also take the shorthand "ra".

resetpos
    resets the position of the graph.
    can also take the shorthand "rp".

resetscale
    resets the scaling of the graph.
    can also take the shorthand "rs".

resetgraph
    resets the scaling and position of the graph.
    can also take the shorthand "rg".

init
    resets the graph, and clears all plots.
    can also take the shorthand "ii".

setres [resolution]
    sets the resolution of the plotter.
    can also take the shorthand "sr".

help [~command]
    lists possible commands if no input is given.
    else, gives some information about a command.

exit / quit
    exits the program.
